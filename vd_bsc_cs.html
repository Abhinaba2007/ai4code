Program Overview

This 3-year undergraduate specialization combines traditional computer science education with cutting-edge artificial intelligence training. The program follows UGC guidelines for BSc Computer Science while incorporating specialized AI coursework, preparing students for careers in AI development, data science, and machine learning engineering.
