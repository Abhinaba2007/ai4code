Program Overview

This Bachelor of Technology program in Artificial Intelligence provides comprehensive training in engineering principles combined with cutting-edge AI technologies. The curriculum covers computer science fundamentals, mathematics for AI, machine learning, deep learning, robotics, and AI system design, preparing students for careers in AI engineering, research, and development.
